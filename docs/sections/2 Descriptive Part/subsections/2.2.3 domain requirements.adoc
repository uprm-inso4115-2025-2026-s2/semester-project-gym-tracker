=== 2.2.3 Domain Requirements

*Session Representation*
    The system shall represent each Workout Session as a distinct entity associated with:
        •	Specific calendar date
        •	Session type
        •	Duration
        •	Exercises Performed

*Scheduling*
    The system shall associate each workout session with a specific calendar date to represent the distribution of sessions across time.

*Persistent Storage and Retrieval*
    The system shall provide means to store Workout Sessions persistently and retrieve them in chronological order.

*Streak Representation*
    The system shall provide means to determine and represent sequences of consecutive Workout sessions across days or weeks as a Streak.

*Streak Calculation*
    The system shall compute whether a Workout session continues or breaks an existing Streak based on consecutive calendar units.

*Weekly Aggregation*
    The system shall provide means to aggregate workout sessions within calendar weeks

*Weekly Comparison*
    The system shall provide means to compare the number of sessions performed in the current week with previous weeks

*Essential Information Recording*
    The system shall provide means to record a Workout Session using only essential information: session type, duration, and optional short notes.

*Separation of Nutrition Tracking*
    The system shall not require Nutrition information as a precondition for recording a Workout Session.
