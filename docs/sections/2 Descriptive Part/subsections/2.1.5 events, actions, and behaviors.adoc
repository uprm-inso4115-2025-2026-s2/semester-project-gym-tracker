=== 2.1.5 Events, Actions, and Behaviors

==== Domain Events

The following events are observable from the stakeholder perspective
and occur at specific moments in time.

*Workout session has just been completed*  
A person has finished performing one or more activities intended for fitness.

*Workout session has just been recorded*  
A completed workout has been added to the activity history.

*Workout day has just been missed*  
A day expected to include activity has passed without a workout session.

*Streak has just been extended*  
A workout has occurred on a day immediately following another attendance day.

*Streak has just been broken*  
A missed day has occurred following one or more consecutive attendance days.

*Weekly goal period has just ended*  
The time interval associated with a weekly goal has completed.

*Weekly activity summary has just been evaluated*  
The completed weekâ€™s activity has been compared against its goal.

==== Actions

*Perform workout*  
A person engages in physical activity such as exercising, running, or lifting.

*Record workout*  
A person logs details of a completed workout session.

*Set activity goal*  
A person defines a target amount of activity for a future time period.

*Review progress*  
A person inspects their past activity and current consistency.

==== Behaviors

*Maintaining a workout routine*  
A repeated pattern of performing workouts across days or weeks.

*Building consistency*  
A gradual increase in regularity of attendance over time.

*Tracking progress over time*  
A repeated cycle of performing workouts, recording them, and reviewing outcomes.
